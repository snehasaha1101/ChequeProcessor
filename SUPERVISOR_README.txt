Cheque Processing & OCR Portal
==============================

To run:
  1. Unzip this folder anywhere (e.g. your Desktop).
  2. Open the folder and double-click  ChequeProcessor.exe
  3. A console window opens, then your browser opens to the app.
     (If the browser doesn't open, go to  http://127.0.0.1:7860 )
  4. Upload a cheque image, pick a detector and OCR engine(s), and run.

Notes:
  - First launch takes 30-60 seconds while the models load. This is normal.
  - Keep the black console window open while using the app; closing it
    stops the app. Close it when you're finished.
  - Everything runs locally on your machine. No internet is required.
